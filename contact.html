Njör is a microbrewery created by craft beer enthusiasts for craft beer enthusiasts. This Scandinavian-based microbrewery got its name from Njöror, a Norse God of seafaring and crop fertility. At its roots, Njör is committed to handcrafting flavorful brews with quality ingredients and thoughtful recipes.
